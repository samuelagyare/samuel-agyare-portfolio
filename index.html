About Me

Architecting Secure, Automated, and Compliant Cloud Systems

Shipping new features is essential, but doing it without introducing security vulnerabilities or risking downtime often feels impossible. I solve this. As a Google Professional Cloud Security Engineer, I build and automate secure cloud infrastructure that embeds security into the delivery pipeline from day one, resulting in faster, safer deployments and more resilient systems.

My approach has delivered measurable business results, including a 70% faster infrastructure provisioning time with Terraform, a 40% reduction in production misconfigurations through robust DevSecOps controls, and a 30% faster incident resolution time (MTTR) using proactive observability stacks on Google Cloud. With hands-on experience in ISO 27001 and secure GKE deployments, I bridge the gap between technical execution and strategic business objectives.

If you're looking for an expert partner to build a secure and efficient cloud foundation that enables your business, I'm here to help.
